About Me

Turning Complex Problems Into Elegant Solutions

I bridge the gap between business needs and technical implementation, delivering software that scales with your growth.

I recently graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) at STI College Dasmariñas, where I developed a full-stack salon appointment system as my thesis project. This system featured an AI-powered customer inquiry assistant using Chatbase API, and an admin panel built with PHP, MySQL, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap

I also gained hands-on experience as a Game Developer Intern at GoCrayons Digital Inc., where I designed and developed 2D games using Godot Engine and GDScript, implemented core mechanics, and collaborated with a team in an agile environment.

My technical toolkit includes Java, Python, C#, PHP, React, Tailwind CSS, Bootstrap, MySQL, and SQL Server. I am passionate about full-stack development, game development, and creating user-friendly systems. I’m eager to keep learning and contribute to projects that challenge me to grow as a developer.
